Transforming Leadership has a shared vision and strategy. We are an empowering income exempt charity with a development strategy aimed at transforming leader's lives. We aim to enhance their own learning and facilitate their ability to transform the lives of others in the communities from which they come. A diagrammatic illustration of our Concept Development Strategy can be seen at this link.

Our Vision

Transforming Leadership exists to equip leaders from all walks of life to be trained in academic institutions that provide progressive, practical and scholarly courses in leadership. Transforming Leadership strives to empower these leaders enabling them to develop projects that will improve the lives of people affected by poverty, famine and disease.

Our Mission

Members and friends of Transforming Leadership endorse our mission to train leaders to model their lives and leadership style on Jesus Christ who was anointed:

"... to preach good news to the poor …to proclaim freedom for the prisoners and recovery of sight for the blind, to release the oppressed and to proclaim the year of the Lord's favour" (Luke 4:18, 19).


We see the mandate of a leader is to emulate St Paul's admonition to:

"... be transformed by the renewing of your mind in order to be able to test and approve what God's will is – His good, pleasing and perfect will" (Romans 12:2).


Our motivation is to see godly leaders transformed in their ability to lead, exhibiting a style of renewed leadership that is marked by:

"... godliness with contentment” and the pursuit of “… righteousness, godliness, faith, love, endurance and gentleness" (1 Timothy 6:6 and 6:11).


These are the essential qualities of a godly leader.


Our Primary Objective

Many people in rural communities in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ghana and Sierra Leone are vulnerable and disadvantaged despite decades of humanitarian effort and expenditure.

The primary objective of Transforming Leadership is to provide scholarships for enhancing the skills of leaders and enabling them to empower others with communication skills, management skills and leadership.

Transforming Leadership facilitates the organisation of seminars, courses and projects that improve the lives of those people affected by poverty, famine and disease. We seek to cooperate with other organisations so as to effectively fulfil the primary objective.

 

Current Specific Priorities

Over the past decade, Transforming Leadership has a proven record of sponsored graduates fulfilling its primary objective in the Congo and Ghana in the provision of health services, education and employment. Important health services are being currently developed in Sierra Leone in order to address the Ebola epidemic and other serious communicable diseases.

The top strategic priority for 2015 - 2016 is to expand the number of leaders being trained to provide practical sustainable services to the poor in:

  • three communities in the Congo
  • five communities in Ghana
  • one community in Sierra Leone
