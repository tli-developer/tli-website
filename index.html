Transforming Leadership

Transforming Leadership exists to equip leaders from all walks of life to be trained in academic institutions that provide progressive, practical and scholarly courses in leadership. Transforming Leadership strives to empower these leaders enabling them to develop projects that will improve the lives of people affected by poverty, famine and disease.

We partner with specific organisations in sub-Saharan Africa. Currently we are providing scholarships and supporting projects in Ghana, the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Sierra Leone. We are looking to expand to other areas in Africa and beyond as financing becomes available. If you would like to find out more about Transforming Leadership, please feel free to contact us.
